Overall Conclusion on competition page template design research

- fancy templates are not suitable and may provide negative experience because of too many bells & whistles distracting from actual content the user come for. 

- Website layout optimzed for information consumption through images, clean visual representation may be preferred.

- Simple layout like visitlondon, myswitzerland are preferred. 

- make only article with sidebar, category landing page, place page, listicle article template which should serve all the pages with slight base modifications. 

- this leaves to go with pagedone or agon only.
