## Autumn in Kashmir: Golden Landscapes and Serene Beauty

Autumn is one of the most visually stunning seasons in Kashmir. As summer fades, the valley turns golden with **Chinar trees**, clear skies, and crisp air. This season is less crowded, peaceful, and perfect for travelers who prefer calm and scenic beauty.

Autumn in Kashmir is ideal for photographers, couples, and slow travelers.
