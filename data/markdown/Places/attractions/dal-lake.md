![Dal Lake Srinagar](/img/attractions/dal-lake.jpg)

Dal Lake is one of the most iconic attractions in Kashmir, renowned for its shimmering waters, traditional houseboats, and breathtaking Himalayan backdrop. Located along **Boulevard Road in Srinagar**, the lake is a cultural and scenic highlight of Jammu & Kashmir.

Visitors can enjoy peaceful **shikara rides**, explore the famous **floating vegetable markets**, and stay overnight in beautifully crafted **wooden houseboats**. Surrounded by Mughal gardens, local handicraft markets, and mountain views, Dal Lake offers unforgettable experiences throughout the day. Early morning reflections and sunset rides make it a favorite spot for photographers and nature lovers.

## üöï Travel Information

Dal Lake is easily accessible by **taxi or auto-rickshaw** from anywhere in Srinagar. The lake stretches along Boulevard Road, making multiple access points convenient for visitors.

The **best time to visit is from April to October**, when the weather is pleasant and shikara rides operate throughout the day. Sunset rides are especially popular and recommended.

For those planning to stay overnight, **houseboat bookings should be made in advance**, especially during peak tourist seasons. Nearby attractions include **Nishat Garden, Shalimar Garden, and Shankaracharya Hill**.
