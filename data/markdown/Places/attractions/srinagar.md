Srinagar, nestled in the heart of the **Kashmir Valley**, is renowned for its pristine lakes, traditional houseboats, and lush Mughal gardens. As the summer capital of Jammu & Kashmir, the city offers a perfect blend of natural beauty, cultural richness, and historical charm.

Visitors can enjoy peaceful **shikara rides on Dal Lake and Nigeen Lake**, explore vibrant floating markets, and admire the architectural elegance of Mughal gardens such as **Shalimar Bagh, Nishat Bagh, and Chashme Shahi**. The old city showcases traditional Kashmiri life with historic mosques, temples, wooden houses, and bustling bazaars. Srinagar is a paradise for photography lovers, leisure travelers, and cultural explorers, offering unforgettable experiences in every season.

## üöï Travel Information

Srinagar is **well-connected by both road and air**. The nearest airport is **Srinagar International Airport**, from where taxis and cabs are easily available for city travel. Local transport options include taxis, autos, and shared vehicles.

The **best time to visit Srinagar is from April to October**, when the weather is pleasant and lakes remain fully navigable. During this period, visitors can enjoy shikara rides, sightseeing, and garden visits comfortably. Major attractions include **Dal Lake, Nigeen Lake, Shalimar Bagh, Nishat Bagh, Chashme Shahi**, and the lively old city markets. Travelers can also experience authentic **Kashmiri cuisine** at local cafes and traditional restaurants across the city.
