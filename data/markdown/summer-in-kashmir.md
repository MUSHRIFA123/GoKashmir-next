## Summer in Kashmir: Pleasant Weather and Lush Green Valleys

Summer in Kashmir is the most popular season for tourists, offering cool weather, breathtaking landscapes, and endless sightseeing opportunities. While most parts of India experience extreme heat, Kashmir remains refreshingly pleasant, making it a perfect summer escape. Snow melts completely in most areas, revealing lush valleys, alpine meadows, and crystal-clear rivers.

From April to June, Kashmir becomes a haven for families, honeymooners, and adventure seekers alike.
