Aru Valley is a serene and pristine destination located near **Pahalgam**, known for its lush meadows, dense pine forests, and breathtaking natural landscapes. Tucked away from the busy tourist spots, the valley offers a peaceful atmosphere ideal for travelers seeking nature, adventure, and relaxation.

The valley serves as an important **gateway for trekking, camping, and nature excursions** in Kashmir. Visitors can enjoy **pony rides, riverside walks**, and quiet moments surrounded by snow-capped mountains and open green meadows. Aru Valley is especially popular among photographers and adventure lovers who want to experience an authentic and less-crowded side of Kashmir.

## üöï Travel Information

Aru Valley is located about **12 km from Pahalgam** and can be reached by **taxi or shared cab**. The road journey offers beautiful views of rivers, forests, and mountain scenery.

The **best time to visit Aru Valley is from April to October**, when the weather is pleasant and trekking routes remain accessible. During winter months, the region experiences **heavy snowfall**, which may limit road access and outdoor activities. Visitors planning treks or camping should check local weather conditions in advance.
