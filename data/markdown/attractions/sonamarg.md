## üöï Travel Information

Sonamarg is easily accessible by **road from Srinagar** via taxis and shared cabs. The drive itself is scenic, passing through forests, rivers, and mountain terrain.

The **best time to visit Sonamarg is from May to October**, when roads are open and the weather is suitable for sightseeing and outdoor activities. During winter months, heavy snowfall may block access roads.

Visitors are advised to start early in the day to avoid traffic and weather changes. Pony services are available for glacier visits, and warm clothing is recommended even in summer due to cold winds.
