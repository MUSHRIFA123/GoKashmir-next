Pahalgam, a peaceful town nestled in the Kashmir Valley, along with the nearby **Betaab Valley**, offers breathtaking landscapes filled with lush meadows, dense pine forests, and flowing rivers. The scenic **Lidder River** winds through the region, enhancing its natural charm and creating perfect settings for relaxation and photography.

Betaab Valley, named after a famous Bollywood movie, is especially admired for its cinematic beauty and tranquil environment. Visitors can enjoy nature walks, riverside picnics, and panoramic views of snow-capped mountains. Together, Pahalgam and Betaab Valley provide a refreshing escape for nature lovers, trekkers, photographers, and adventure enthusiasts seeking serenity amidst stunning Himalayan scenery.

## üöï Travel Information

Pahalgam is located approximately **95 km from Srinagar** and can be reached by **taxi or bus**. The journey itself offers scenic views of valleys and rivers.

Betaab Valley is accessible via a **short drive from Pahalgam**, with pony rides available for sightseeing within the valley. The **best time to visit is from April to October**, when the weather is pleasant and ideal for outdoor activities. Summers are comfortable, while winter months may bring snowfall, adding a different charm but limiting access.
