Tulip Garden Srinagar, officially known as the **Indira Gandhi Memorial Tulip Garden**, is Asia‚Äôs largest tulip garden and a spectacular spring attraction in Kashmir. Nestled on the slopes of the **Zabarwan Range near Cheshma Shahi**, the garden comes alive with thousands of tulip varieties blooming in vibrant colors.

During the spring season, the garden transforms into a visual paradise, offering breathtaking floral landscapes set against the majestic Himalayan backdrop. Visitors can enjoy peaceful walks along well-maintained pathways, capture stunning photographs, and immerse themselves in the refreshing atmosphere. The garden also hosts the **annual Tulip Festival**, attracting tourists and nature enthusiasts from across India and around the world.

## üöï Travel Information

Tulip Garden Srinagar is easily accessible by **taxi or private vehicle** from anywhere in the city. The garden is located about **12 km from central Srinagar**, making it a convenient day visit.

The **best time to visit is from March to April**, when the tulips are in full bloom. The garden remains open from **morning until 7:00 pm**. Visiting early in the day is recommended to avoid crowds and enjoy softer natural light for photography.
